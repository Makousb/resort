The Kokomo Resort

Set amidst 725 acres of breathtaking tropical beauty in Cocomo’s northern island of Cono levu, Kokomo Resort & Spa is redefining all-inclusive. Our intimate, adults-only boutique setting offers a truly unique and authentically Cocomoian experience where your every request is met, every want anticipated, and every expectation exceeded. Catering to some of the world’s most exclusive clientele from actors Anthony Hopkins, Edward Norton, and Russell Crowe to fashion icon Donna Karan and NBA Coach Pat Riley, our world-class staff is devoted to providing you an exquisitely luxurious experience unparalleled by any other all-inclusive resort in Cocomo.
